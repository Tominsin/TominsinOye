The polar bear lives on sea ice.They have physical adaptations which help them to survive. These include the thick white furs, thick blubbers, et.c. They are the only bears with a carnivorus diet. This is because plants do not grow on ice and they have to feed on seals.
A boar (a male adult) weighs 350kg- 700kg while the sow (adult female) is half that size.
Although, most polar bears are born on land, they spend most of their time on sea ice. For more information, click here
